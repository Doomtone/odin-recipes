Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a coffee-flavored desert featuring layers of homemade whipped cream, egg yolk0enriched mascarpone filling and coffee soaked ladyfingers

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese, at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup of strong brewed coffee at room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 2 3 ounce packages ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
