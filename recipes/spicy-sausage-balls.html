Spicy Sausage Balls

These sausage balls are as good as the Bisquick sausage balls, but with out the carbs or gluten.
Thy are also keto friendly! Eat them fresh out of the oven or refrigerate or freeze them for late use.

  • 1 pound of bulk spicy pork sausage
  • 1 pound of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of almond flour
  • 2 tablespoons coconut flour
  • 2 (.25 ounce) packages unflavored gelatin
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon backing powder
