Spicy Sausage Balls

Description

These sausage balls are as good as the Bisquick sausage balls, but with out the carbs or gluten.
Thy are also keto friendly! Eat them fresh out of the oven or refrigerate or freeze them for late use.

ingredients

  • 1 pound of bulk spicy pork sausage
  • 1 pound of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of almond flour
  • 2 tablespoons coconut flour
  • 2 (.25 ounce) packages unflavored gelatin
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon backing powder

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Line a jelly roll pan with parchment paper.
  2. Grab a pan to put the final mixture in. Put some oil in the pan to make sure thy wont stick to the pan.
  3. Combine sausage, cheddar cheese, almond flour, coconut flour, gelatin, egg and backing powder in a large bowl.
    Mix to combine using your hands, a stand mixer or food processor. Form mixture in uniform sized balls about 1 1/2-inch diameter
    and place in the prepared pan
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked for about 23 to 28 minutes. When thy are done remove the sausage ball from the oven,
    leave the balls in the pan to cool off and wait until all the oil has been reabsorbed with in about 5 minutes.
  5. Finally put them on a plate and serve them to your guests, or treat yourself to these spicy sausage balls
