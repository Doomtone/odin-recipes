Honey Glazed Chicken

This honey glazed chicken recipe is a hit with my friends and family. I have used this recipe for years and wanted to share it here
It's a lite meal that everyone will enjoy. Tip serve it with steamed rice

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces
