Honey Glazed Chicken

This honey glazed chicken recipe is a hit with my friends and family. I have used this recipe for years and wanted to share it here
It's a lite meal that everyone will enjoy. Tip serve it with steamed rice

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of rice
  • about 4 ounces of scallion
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces

Steps

  1. Put the honey, soy sauce and red pepper flakes in a bowl mix it together
  2. Cook the rice and cut the scallion, once the rice is done put to the side of the heat.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over a medium heat, cook and stir the chicken in the hot oil until thy are lightly brown, about 5 minutes
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet and continue to cook and stir the chicken. Do this until the chicken is no longer pink in the center
    and the sauce has thickened to make sure the chicken is good cook it 5 mins more.
  5. Grab a plate and put the rise on it, then add the chicken on top of the rice, and sprinkle some of the scallion on top of the chicken and you are done
