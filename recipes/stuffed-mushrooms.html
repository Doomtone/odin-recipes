Stuffed Mushrooms

Description

These delicious stuffed mushrooms are pretty easy to make and it will only take about 20 minutes,
and thy taste just like the once in the restaurants.

ingredients

  • 12 whole fresh mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a backing sheet with cooking spray.
    Clean mushrooms with a damp paper towel, carefully break off the stems. Chop stems extremely fine,
    discarding the tough end of the stems.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over a medium head. add garlic and chopped stems,
    fry until any moisture has evaporated. Taking care not to burn the garlic. Set aside to cool off.
  3. Stir in cream cheese, parmesan cheese, black pepper, onion powder and cayenne pepper.
    The mixture will be very thick, use a teaspoon to full each mushroom cap with a generous amount fo stuffing.
    Arrange mushroom caps on a prepared cookie sheet
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the mushrooms are piping hot, in about 20 minutes.
