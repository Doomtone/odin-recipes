Classic Carpaccio

This is our classic Carpaccio with Arugula

The classic Carpaccio is a great dish as a appetizer. This cold appetizer from Venice with thin sliced fillet of beef.
This dish is delightful to serve during christmas, and other occasions of course.
Try not to add too much Arugula, that is what restaurants usually do. Try to keep it pure.

The ingredient's

  • 160 g fillet of beef
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese
  • 20 g roasted pine nuts
  • 1/2 lemon (juice)
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 15 ml balsamic vinegar
  • 10 ml Dijon mustard
  • A small handful of Arugula

Steps

  1. Cut the 160 g fillet of beef in very thin slices. Or ask your Butcher to do this
  2. Make the vinaigrette by mixing 30 ml olive oil, 15 ml balsamic vinegar,
    add the lemon juice and 10 ml Dijon mustard. Finally add salt and pepper to your own taste.
  3. Take 2 plates and divide the beef between the 2 plates
  4. Per plate add a small handful of Arugula, with some grated parmesan cheese and roasted pine nuts
  5. Drizzle the vinaigrette on the 2 plates and finally add some salt and pepper
